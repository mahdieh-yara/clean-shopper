Clean Shopper

Clean Shopper feels like consulting a calm, knowledgeable friend who has already done the research for you. The interface is minimal and quiet — soft off-white and white surfaces layered for depth, generous breathing room, rounded approachable shapes, and editorial typography that feels considered rather than loud.

A single muted leaf green carries the brand and signals trust without shouting. The rest of the palette stays near-monochrome and cool, warmed by sand-toned neutrals. Organic without being precious, approachable without being playful, and explicitly not luxurious, glossy, or beauty-fashion.

Principles

Five rules of thumb

Specific to Clean Shopper. Every later decision traces back to these.

Quiet by default

No urgency, no conversion pressure, no visual noise. The interface stays still so the information can speak.

Reassurance through restraint

Trust is communicated by what's absent — no exclamation marks, no shouting badges, no aggressive contrast.

Depth through surface

Hierarchy comes from stacking off-white, white, and warm sand surfaces. Shadows are reserved for floating UI.

Organic warmth in a cool palette

Cool off-whites for considered tone. Warm sand neutrals for humanity. Neither clinical nor boutique.

Rounded, not playful

Pill CTAs and generous card radii signal approachability — type, spacing, and motion stay editorial.

Dark Mode

Same system, inverted

Light mode is home; dark mode is a supported alternative. The dark palette inverts surface hierarchy while preserving the warm-cool tension and muted brand green.
